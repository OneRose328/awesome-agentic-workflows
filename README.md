# Awesome Agentic Workflows

Welcome to the Awesome Agentic Workflows repository! This repository is dedicated to providing a comprehensive collection of workflows that you can use to enhance your productivity and create efficient systems in your daily life and work. Below, you'll find our complete list of templates organized into categories and a quick start guide for getting started.

## Categories

### Personal Development
1. **Morning Routine**: A structured morning routine to start your day off right.
2. **Goal Setting**: Templates for setting and tracking your personal goals.
3. **Meditation and Mindfulness**: Workflows for practicing meditation and mindfulness.

### Project Management
1. **Task Management**: A workflow to prioritize and manage tasks effectively.
2. **Team Collaboration**: Templates for improving team communication and collaboration.
3. **Deadline Tracker**: Keep track of important deadlines with this workflow.

### Health and Wellness
1. **Fitness Tracker**: Log your exercise routines and track your progress.
2. **Meal Planning**: Plan your meals for the week to ensure healthy eating.
3. **Sleep Tracker**: Monitor your sleep patterns for better health.

### Finance
1. **Budget Tracker**: Keep track of your income and expenses.
2. **Investment Planner**: Guidelines for planning your investments.
3. **Savings Goals**: Templates for setting and achieving savings goals.

### Creativity
1. **Brainstorming**: Capture ideas and develop them further.
2. **Writing Planner**: Organize your writing projects and deadlines.
3. **Design Inspiration**: Gather design ideas for your projects.

## Quick Start Guide

To get started with your workflows, follow these simple steps:
1. **Choose a category** that fits your needs.
2. **Select a template** from that category.
3. **Customize the template** to match your specific requirements.
4. **Implement the workflow** in your daily routines.
